1. Terms of Service

FAWN ("we," "us") is a small independent shop selling curated vintage and second-hand clothing. By placing an order you ("you," "the buyer") agree to these terms. If anything here is unclear, email us before you order — we'd rather get it right.

About our items

  • Every item is second-hand and one of one. Once it sells, it's gone.
  • We describe condition honestly, including any flaws, and photograph the actual item you'll receive — never a stock photo.
  • Sizes are listed as labeled; vintage sizing varies, so we also give measurements where we can. Ask if you need more.
  • Colors may look slightly different between screens. We don't consider minor screen-color variation a defect.

Placing an order

  • Adding an item to your bag does not reserve it. An item is reserved for 30 minutes once you complete checkout, to give you time to pay.
  • If payment isn't received within 30 minutes, the item is released and may sell to someone else.
  • We confirm every order by email with an order code (e.g. FAWN-7K3Q) you can use to track it.

2. Payment & buyer protection

We want you to feel safe spending money with a small seller you've just met. So we're upfront about how each payment method protects you:

PayPal — Goods & Services (recommended). When you pay this way, PayPal's Purchase Protection covers you: if your item never arrives, or arrives significantly not as described, you can open a dispute with PayPal and get your money back. Always choose "Goods & Services," not "Friends & Family" — Friends & Family has no protection. Send payment to jessicapotterrr@gmail.com with your order code in the note.
Apple Cash — local, in-person pickup only. Apple Cash is a person-to-person transfer with no buyer or refund protection of any kind. Because of that, we only accept it for in-person local handoffs, where you can see the item before you pay. We do not ship items paid by Apple Cash. For any mailed order, please use PayPal Goods & Services so you're protected.

We will never ask you to pay outside these methods, send extra money "for shipping insurance," or pay a second time. If anyone claiming to be us asks for that, it's a scam — stop and email us at the address below.

Prices are in US dollars. You pay the item price plus shipping shown at checkout. We don't add hidden fees.

3. Getting your item — meet up or local shipping

At checkout you choose how to receive your piece:

Meet up — local pickup (free)

  • After you order, we message you to agree on a time and a safe, public meeting place (think a busy coffee shop or store entrance — never a private home).
  • You can pay in person at the meet-up by Apple Cash or PayPal, or pay ahead by PayPal Goods & Services.
  • Bring your order code. Your order's tracking page shows the steps: received → packed → ready for pickup → picked up.
  • For your safety and ours, meet-ups are in public places only and during daytime hours where possible.

Local shipping (flat $6)

  • Cost: a flat $6, added at checkout.
  • Handling time: paid orders are packed and shipped within 2 business days.
  • Carrier & tracking: we ship via USPS/UPS with tracking. Your tracking number is emailed to you and appears on your tracking page the moment we ship.
  • Estimated delivery: typically 3–6 business days after shipping, depending on the carrier.
  • Shipped orders must be paid by PayPal Goods & Services (not Apple Cash), so you're protected. Once a package is with the carrier, delivery timing is in their hands — we'll always help you chase a lost or delayed package.

4. Returns & refunds

Because every piece is one of one and second-hand, we handle returns carefully and fairly:

We'll make it right if:

  • The item arrives not as described (a flaw or measurement we missed), or
  • The item is damaged in transit, or
  • Your order never arrives.

In any of those cases, email us within 5 days of delivery (or of the expected delivery date) with your order code and a photo if relevant. We'll offer a full refund or replacement. If you paid by PayPal Goods & Services, you're also covered by PayPal's own dispute process.

Sizing & change of mind

  • Because items are one-of-one vintage, we generally sell as-is and don't accept "changed my mind" returns. Please check the listed measurements before buying — and ask us anything first.
  • If you're genuinely unsure on fit, message us before ordering and we'll measure it for you.

How refunds are paid

Approved refunds go back to your original payment method within 3 business days of us approving them. PayPal refunds appear in your PayPal balance or on your card per PayPal's timing.

5. Our delivery promise

This is the part that matters most: you will get what you paid for. Here's exactly how we make sure of it, and how you can hold us to it:

  1. Protected payment. Paying by PayPal Goods & Services means the money is recoverable by you if we ever fail to deliver. That's your safety net, guaranteed by PayPal — not just our word.
  2. Reserved item. Your specific piece is set aside the moment you check out and marked sold so no one else can buy it.
  3. 2-day handling. We pack within 2 business days. If you chose shipping, we then email you a real tracking number; if you chose to meet up, we message you to arrange a time and safe public place.
  4. Live timeline. Your order code shows a step-by-step timeline, updated as it moves — shipping: received → packed → shipped → out for delivery → delivered; pickup: received → packed → ready for pickup → picked up.
  5. If anything goes wrong — lost, late, or not as described — we refund or replace it, and PayPal backs you up.
No tracking after 3 business days? Item not as described? Email jessicapotterrr@gmail.com with your order code and we'll resolve it or refund you. We're a real person and we answer.

  • Email us anytime to see, correct, or delete the information we hold about you.
